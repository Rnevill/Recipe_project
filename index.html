Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Snickerdoodles

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) softened butter
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 pachage milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 package peanut butter chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mix/smash the 1 cup soft butter and the 1 1/2 cups sugar. Stir until mixed very well
  3. Add 2 eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each
  4. Stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. Add the 2 3/4 cup flour, 2 teaspoons baking powder, and 1/4 teaspoon salt gradually
  6. Stir in chocolate and peanut butter chips
  7. Cover and chill for about 15 minutes
  8. In a small bowl, mix 3 tablespoons sugar and 3 teaspoons cinnamon
  9. Roll dough into 1 inch balls, roll them in the sugar and cinnamon mix and place onto cookie sheet
  10. Bake at 350 degrees F for 10 minutes
  11. Let cool and eat
